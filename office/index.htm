Dr. Chuck's Office Hours

This is a map of Dr. Chuck's remote office hours. Because of travel restrictions due to Covid, upcoming office hours are live and online. We have live office hours about once per month that you can join and participate. You can also view past office hours recordings on YouTube.

On the map, the most recent remote office hour is always at the center of the map. Sometimes you need to zoom in quite closely when there are more than one office hour video for a particular city (i.e. Amsterdam, Bletchley Park, New York City, or Seoul).

Dr. Chuck teaches a number of popular online courses including Python for Everybody, Django for Everybody, PostgreSQL for Everybody, Web Applications for Everybody (PHP and MySQL). Dr. Chuck teaches courses on Coursera, edX, FutureLearn and his free open materials are used by many schools and teachers around the world.

My video that includes Paris and Milan was my fun attempt to film one of these visits in the style of a Travel Channel program.
